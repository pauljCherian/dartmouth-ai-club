From Our Constitution

"Dartmouth AI Club is a student-driven community where undergraduates explore the frontiers of artificial intelligence. We're committed to making AI accessible, fostering hands-on learning, and building a supportive network of curious minds ready to shape the future of technology."

Membership

Open to all members of the Dartmouth community. Attend two consecutive meetings to become a member.

Elections

Officers elected by majority vote during the third week of spring term.

Meetings

Regular meetings held at least twice per term, with at least one day's notice to all members.
