Who We Are

Our Community

Dartmouth AI Club is a student-driven organization bringing together undergraduates passionate about artificial intelligence. We've built a welcoming community where students can dive into cutting-edge AI topics, share ideas, and learn from each other‚Äîno prior experience required.

Weekly Sessions

Each week, members lead discussions on exciting AI breakthroughs‚Äîfrom generative models and computer vision to the latest in LLMs and reinforcement learning. We also organize speaker events, hands-on workshops, and collaborative projects that bridge classroom theory with real-world applications.

Become a Member

Getting involved is simple‚Äîjust show up! We welcome everyone from complete beginners to experienced practitioners. Come to two meetings in a row to become an official member, or just drop by whenever a topic catches your interest. Sign up for our mailing list to stay in the loop.

Academic Connections

Our club partners with Dartmouth's Computer Science department and benefits from guidance by a dedicated faculty advisor. These connections open doors to research opportunities, academic insights, and networking with professors working on groundbreaking AI projects.

"We believe AI literacy should be accessible to every Dartmouth student. Our goal is to demystify artificial intelligence through peer learning, hands-on exploration, and a supportive community where curiosity drives discovery."
